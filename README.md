# Catchit – Mobile App UI Design

## Project Overview

Catchit is a school transport safety app concept designed to help parents and students track school bus routes, check stop updates, and stay informed about safe school travel.

The main idea behind Catchit is to reduce the daily confusion parents face around school buses. For example, if a child misses the bus, parents may not know whether the bus has already left, which stop the bus has reached, whether the bus is delayed, or if another approved school bus is available on the same route.

Catchit helps parents track the current bus, check the next stop, receive route updates, and stay informed about safer school travel options.

## App Concept

Catchit focuses on reducing daily confusion for parents and students by helping them know:

* Where the school bus currently is
* Whether the bus has reached or crossed the next stop
* If the bus is running late
* If the child has missed the bus
* Whether another approved school bus is available on the same route
* How parents can stay updated with safer school travel information

The goal is to make school transport tracking simple, safe, and stress-free for parents, students, and schools.

## Problem Statement

Parents often face uncertainty when their child misses the school bus or when the bus is delayed. They may not know the current bus location, the next stop, or whether another school bus is available on the same route.

Catchit aims to solve this problem by providing school bus tracking, stop updates, delay information, and safer alternate route visibility.
